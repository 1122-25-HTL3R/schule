## Blog Post
### Eco-Friendly-Workplaces

As we all know, our climate is in great danger at the moment. Tons of greenhouse gasses are released into our atmosphere every hour. We at MKE have to find and implement ways to be more sustainable, since we as a company cannot afford to harm our planet even more.

There are lots of different ways to be more eco-friendly as a company. For example, we could install solar panels on our buildings roofs and switch to a more sustainable energy provider. This would be a change that could easily be implemented from our current standpoint. 

The company could negotiate deals with the public transport commitee to get employees a bonus for their public transport tickets. Trains and busses are great ways to leave a smaller ecological footprint behind.But even if every singele employee came to work via public transport the companies CO2 output wouldnt even change 5%.

We have to make changes to the way we produce our products if we want to make an impact in the companies CO2-footprint. We could make our products more recyclable or work with other companies to make our products compatible with each other. Customers wouldnt have to buy extra accescories for every new product.

In conclusion: There are many Methods to reduce our companies CO2 output. We could switch our energy provider or change the way we produce our products.
